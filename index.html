Kevin Wang

Writer & Translator

Kevin Wang is a Taipei-based writer and educator. He is the translator of Terao Tetsuya's Spent Bullets (HarperVia, 2025) and a co-translator of Chi Ta-wei's History of Tongzhi Literature, Kan's Hysteria, and Kathy Lam's Mobu's Diary. His translations have appeared in Gulf Coast, Circumference, The Margins, and Asymptote.

He holds degrees from Skidmore College and Columbia University, where he taught undergraduate writing. He works in professional development for Fulbright Taiwan.
